# Regional_Airline_Cost-Pricing_Strategy_Analysis_under_Fuel_Price_Shock

– Evaluated fuel price increase (10%) and its effect on operating cost.

– Analyzed fuel contribution to total cost and resulting margin pressure.

– Assessed profitability across route types (core, regional, marginal, experimental) based on demand elasticity

– Compared three strategic options: Network Optimization Dynamic Pricing, Government backed expansion (UDAN
subsidies), Structural operating model redesign

– Identified risks including demand shift to rail/bus, reduced load factor and long term cash burn

– Recommended network optimization with dynamic pricing as the most balanced strategy

– Proposed risk mitigation strategies such as fuel hedging, route rationalization and fleet optimization
